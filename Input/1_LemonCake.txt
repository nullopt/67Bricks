1. Lemon Cake by Sam Thompson [2018-12-03]

This is a wonderfully delicious recipe for cake.

Ingredients:
225g unsalted butter
225g caster sugar
4 eggs
225g self-raising flour
1 lemon zested

Method:
1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees C.
2. Beat together butter and sugar.
3. Mix in eggs, ont at a time.
4. Sift in flour and add lemon zest.
5. Line loaf time with graseproof paper and spoon mixture in
6. Bake for 45-50 minutes.
